About Me

I am the owner of OSO Web Studio, a web development and marketing agency that I founded in 2009 with the mission of transforming brands with cutting-edge digital solutions. With over 15 years of experience in the field, I have successfully launched and grown hundreds of websites, mobile apps, and ecommerce platforms for clients in the New York tri-state area, nationally and globally.

In addition to running my own agency, I also work as a web developer at Webedia Movies Pro, where I oversee 300+ movie theater websites and a movie database that feeds data to industry leaders such as BoxOfficePro, AMC, Google, and IMDB. I have re-written the in-house CMS system to streamline the data flow and make it scalable for client requests. I have also developed a movie matching mechanism for various inner company databases. Furthermore, I am a digital project manager at Adam House Marketing, where I coordinate and execute web strategy, SEO, social media, and analytics for agency clients with project values ranging from $20k to $500k.

My core competencies include web development, analytics, SEO, social media, project management, and new business development. I have a background in computer science from Northeastern University and I am certified in Agile, Project Management for Creative Projects, and Web Analytics by LinkedIn. I am fluent in English, Russian, and Ukrainian, and have a passion for creating engaging and functional digital experiences that meet the needs and expectations of my customers and their audiences.
